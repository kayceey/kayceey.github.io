摸鱼第3天

第一章

在公司摸鱼第一天，研究了dataphin的用法，然后体会到在互联网公司工作的必备生存技能之优雅摸鱼大法的重要性
I studied the use of dataphin at the first day working in this company. Then i suddenly realised that the importance of learning how to kill time in a proper way.

第二章

在公司摸鱼二天，没有什么实质性产出，我体会到一时摸鱼一时爽，一直摸鱼火葬场。然后我意识到摸鱼的真谛其实是如何合理地以另一种取悦自己的方式去消磨时间。
I did nothing on the second day and i realised that it was interesting to kill time in my own way for a short time. While when having a long time to do the same thing, it was a quite struggle and tough thing for me. And i found the core is
to plan your time to do something you like properly.

第三章

在公司摸鱼第三天，身边的同事也开始意识到摸鱼的枯燥，不过我觉得我开始逐渐学会快乐地摸鱼。
Other friends also felt boring when they have to kill time by themselves, but i realised that i could braze that kind of life and felt happy.
